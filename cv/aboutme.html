I honestly can’t say I always dreamed of going into Computer Science. Since I was sure college wasn’t in the cards for me, I never bothered with dreaming about what I wanted to do. When I was younger, I was slated to take over the family business in flooring. My father built this business from the ground up with no high school education, let alone college, and some of my favorite childhood memories are spent working alongside him carpeting a hotel or a mansion. “Work as best as you can with your god-given ability”, he would say, “financial recompense is merely a by-product”.
